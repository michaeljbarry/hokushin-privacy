Privacy Policy

Kawaii Japanese Flash Cards

Kawaii Japanese Flash Cards is a language-learning app for practicing Japanese vocabulary and study progress. This policy explains what information the app uses and how that information is handled.

Effective May 16, 2026 Applies to Kawaii Japanese Flash Cards

Short version: Kawaii Japanese Flash Cards is designed for private study. The app does not sell personal data and does not use your study activity for third-party advertising.

Information the App Uses

The app may store learning information you create or generate through normal use, including selected decks, study progress, favorite cards, quiz results, review history, streaks, and app preferences.

If the app allows you to create or edit cards, it may also store the Japanese words, translations, examples, notes, or other study content you enter.

Storage

Study data is intended to be stored on your device so the app can preserve your progress and settings. If your device uses Apple system services such as device backup or iCloud backup, Apple may store backup copies according to your Apple ID and device settings.

The app does not require you to create a separate account with the developer.

Analytics, Ads, and Tracking

The app does not sell personal data. The app does not use personal data for third-party advertising.

If optional Apple diagnostics or crash reports are enabled on your device, Apple may provide developers with aggregated or diagnostic information according to Apple's privacy settings. These reports are used to improve app stability and do not provide direct access to your study content.

Purchases

If the app offers paid downloads, in-app purchases, or subscriptions, payment and purchase processing is handled by Apple. The developer does not receive your full payment card details.

Notifications

If you enable study reminders, the app may use local notifications or Apple notification services to remind you to practice. You can manage notification permissions in system settings.

Retention and Deletion

Study data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, reset app data, or remove the app. Backup copies may remain in Apple system backups until those backups are updated or deleted through your Apple account settings.

Children's Privacy

Kawaii Japanese Flash Cards is a language-learning app and is not designed to collect personal contact information from children. If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal information, they can contact the developer using the contact information below.

Security

The app relies on Apple platform protections and your device security settings to help protect locally stored data. No security method is perfect, so you should protect your device passcode and Apple ID.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app changes. The effective date at the top of the page will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions about Kawaii Japanese Flash Cards, contact the developer through the public GitHub profile at github.com/michaeljbarry.
