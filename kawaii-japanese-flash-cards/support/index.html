Kawaii Japanese Flash Cards Support

Help for the free, offline iPhone and iPad app for kana, kanji, radicals, numbers, and phrase practice.

Contact

For support, bug reports, or accessibility feedback, contact the developer through the public Michael Barry GitHub profile. Include the app version, device model, iOS version, and steps that reproduce the problem. Do not include private backup files or other sensitive information.

Study progress and backups

Study progress is stored on the device. Settings can export a backup to a location you choose and import a compatible backup later. Keep a backup before reinstalling the app because removing the app deletes its local data.

Audio

Pronunciation uses Apple system speech voices. Available voices and voice downloads are managed in iOS Settings. Audio remains optional and can be disabled in the app.

N5-style and N4-style practice

These decks are general flashcard practice collections. They are not official JLPT products, complete exam curricula, or simulations of the official test.
